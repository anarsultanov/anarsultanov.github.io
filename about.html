About

Hi, I'm Anar.

Software engineer in Stockholm. Co-Founder and CTO at HI Assessments. Over a decade in B2B software engineering: backend systems, distributed architectures, and services running in production. The thread across most of my work: building software that lasts, not shipping fast and moving on.

This blog is where I write about the engineering problems I've had to solve, mostly drawn from that work. The posts are code-first walkthroughs, not think-pieces or industry takes. I publish when I've worked something out that took me longer than I'd hoped, in case it saves the next reader the same hour.

Outside HI, I also build software on the side through my company AES Tech AB.

You can also find me on LinkedIn, X, and GitHub, or reach me through the contact form.
